Homestyle Pancakes

I got this recipe from my mom who got it from her mom who got it from hers and so on and so forth. This recipe has been in my family for generations and it a staple breakfast meal a Sunday Morning. I would to like to share this with the world so please do enjoy

Ingredients

  • Flour: 1.33 Cups
  • Baking Powder: 3 Tablespoons
  • Salt: 3 Teaspoons
  • Sugar: 0.5 Teaspoons
  • Egg: one Large
  • Milk: 1.25 Cups
  • Vanilla Essence: 1 Teaspoons
  • Melted Butter or Soy Oil: 3 Tablespoons

Directions

  1. Stir Flour, baking powder, salt and Sugar together
  2. Beat Egg thoroughly in bowl
  3. Add milk
  4. Make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients and add egg- milk mixture
  5. Add melted butter(oil) and vanilla.
  6. Stir quickly, until ingredients are just mixed and the batter is still lumpy in appearance.
  7. Drop by 1/4 cupfulls. Cook the pancakes until they are filled with bubbles and the under surface is golden brown. Brown other side
    8. Do not turn pancakes more than once
